About

In the world of interconnected Instagram and Facebook, we seemingly have lost touch with those friends who don't show up in our friend feed.

We created this with one simple purpose: Quickly update us on our friends who we haven't seen in a while.

Doug and Justin hacked this together in a few hours. Questions? Just ask.

The code is nothing complidated/special, but it is freely available here: MyFriendsMe Project.
